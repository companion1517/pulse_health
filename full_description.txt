Pulse Health is a unified, private, offline-first health tracker.

FEATURES
• Weight log with goal tracking, trend chart, and BMI
• Fasting timer with 16:8, 18:6, OMAD and custom windows
• Heart rate — manual entry, or automatic measurement using the rear camera and flashlight (photoplethysmography)
• Workout log with type, duration, intensity, and notes
• JSON export / import — take your data anywhere

PRIVACY
• No accounts, no ads, no trackers, no analytics
• Works fully offline — no network permission required
• All data stays on your device in the app's private storage
• Camera and flashlight are used only during an active heart rate measurement; frames are never stored
